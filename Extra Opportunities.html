Experiential Learning

Becoming a software developer will require experience. One way to get experience is by applying for a co-op job. This allows you to gain experience and knowledge that can help you get a full-time job. Click here to look at some software developer co-op jobs.

Another way to gain experience in the software developer world is by participating in career fairs. Career fairs will help you make connections with other people and learn more about whatever the fair is about. Click here to browse upcoming engineering fairs.

Support Groups

There are lots of programs and communities that are out there that can help support students who want to become software developers.

  • Github - a program that allows you to save and share software for yourself and others
  • Stack Overflow - a question and answer website that covers a large variety of programming languages
  • r/programming - a subreddit where you can browse through forums related to programming and talk to others
